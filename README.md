# Thelousyprogrammer.github.io
## Project: Suisse
- - -
Hello Fellow Devs!
This is Project Suisse, a custom date time record (DTR) for all the fellow OJT students. This website is open source for people who have started or later. Here, you can record your reflections, contributions, tools used and also your personal telemetry details. Gain insights and balance the work and academics with 
- - -
### Features:
- **Telemetry System:** Projected Date of Completion, Hour Distribution, and many more to gain insights from input data to perform well and maintain a work-life balance.
- **Local Image Storage:** With a maximum capacity of 10 GB of image storage, your records of OJT documentation kept all in local, zero cloud hosting.
- **PDF Exports:** Export your documentation weekly, or everything at once!
- **Pace Slider:** A slider to measure your OJT hours with a chart targeting when you will achieve the completion mark.
- **Performance Simulator:** Run your hour stint simulations with instant chart response after inserting your prefered hours and working days!
- - -
### Disclaimer:
If you want to modify the software based on the starting and/or completion date, feel free to pull this repository and configure the date. An update for selecting the starting date and configuring the required OJT hours will be provided in the near future. For privacy like-minded people, pull this repo as well and host it locally.
